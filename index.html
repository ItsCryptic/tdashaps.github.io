Instructions

To download a starter project:

  1. Right-click its link.

  2. Then click Save Link As...

  3. On the next screen, click Downloads.

  4. Then click Save.

  5. Go to Twinery.

  6. Click Import from File. Then click Choose File. Find the file in your Downloads folder and double-click to upload it.
